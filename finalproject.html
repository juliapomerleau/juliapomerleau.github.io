My final project

For my final code, I did an interactive story that is catered towards kids
of the elementary school level. It can be read to children of a younger age,
but, if the child would like to interact directly with the story, they should
have decent reading abilities. The story is about life during this pandemic.
I used Khan's images to construct different rooms and ideas. The reader plays
a first person role when speaking to "Momma," and plays a third person role
when speaking directly to the character. Basically, the reader clicks through
the story, and can go through different outcomes depending on the buttons selected.
